Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • Q: How do I import my own audio files?
    A: Open the app and use the “Import” feature to add your local music (iPhone internal storage).
  • Q: Why can some audio not be imported?
    A: It only support .mp3 format audio files.
  • Q: After reinstalling the app, the audio files cannot be played.
    A: You need to delete them and re-import the audio files from your iPhone.
